1. You can Fill the Online Admission Form by clicking at LINK or can purchase from the school reception.
2. Please fill the Admission Form and then pay the registration fee of Rs.500
3. Admission form can be collected from the school between 9 am to 3 pm on working days.
4. The following documents need to be submitted:
► Birth certificate copy
► ID proof (both parent)
► 1 set of Photo of Child, Father and Mother
► Address proof
► Last two years report card (if applicable)
► Original Transfer certificate
5. Our admission officer will contact you to schedule an interaction and/or assessment within 48
working hours from the day of Application Submission.

NOTE-
► The school has an open entry system and admissions are taken on the first-come-first-serve basis.
► Filling of Admission Form( Registration) does not guarantee admission.



Grade XI Online Admission Process
► Communication with Admission Officer, who briefs about school & other information.
► Admission Form to be purchased
► Academic Documents of the learner to be shared —–> (Mark sheet of Grade IX & Grade X half yearly & Pre-board result and Grade X Final Report Card)
► Provisional Admission Form to be filled —> (For choice of stream & subjects, applicable before Grade-X Final Results)
► Online Interaction takes place with Academic Coordinator & Subject Teachers
► Fee Payment is made
► Admission gets confirmed
