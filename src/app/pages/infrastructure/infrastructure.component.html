Smart Classrooms


R M Public School believes in keeping the students in touch with the latest developments by providing them the latest technology. Digital Smart classes are part of this initiative. The digital content is in accordance with the CBSE school curriculum, facilitated by top most experts from the industry. It shall cover all students of I to IX, covering all subjects in 2D and 3D modules to enable students to grasp the abstract and intricate concepts of the subjects and at the same time have an enriched learning experience

The aim is to keep up with the latest trends by using technology to make learning interesting, simple and interactive with trained faculty to enhance conceptual teaching.

Computer Lab


With computer education growing to be an essential part of modern day living, it has become the priority of the school to make students and teachers computer savvy.

The school has 15 Computer labs for the Pre Primary, Primary, Middle & Senior sections with 35 computers each to equip the staff & students. Special courses on animation, graphic designing etc. apart from regular syllabus is imparted. Teachers are given special training in MS Office to bring in more efficiency.

Maths lab


Maths is a foundation skill that students must acquire. It forms the premise of other subjects such as Science, Economics and even more creative ones such as Architecture. It is the vehicle to train students to think, analyze and articulate logically. Hence, it becomes necessary for students to grasp concepts of Maths.

Maths Lab is an innovative platform where multiple teaching and learning aids are available for teachers to explain the various abstract Mathematical concepts and thereby allowing students to develop deep insights of the concepts. Based on the ideology of Learn – Analyze – Build, our Maths Lab features pedagogically appropriate content, where students can learn through activities, hands-on models and simulations to gain holistic understanding.

Science Lab
Composite Lab, Physics/Chemistry/Biology


Laboratory instruction is considered essential because it provides training in observation, supplies detailed information, and arouses pupils' interest. The science laboratory is an important means of instruction in science skills, concepts and cognitive abilities for understanding the nature of science

The school has a well-equipped science lab along with qualified faculties catering to the needs of Physics, Chemistry as well as Biology for high school students.

Infirmary


At R M Public School the well being of learners is of great importance to us. We provide direct nursing services to learners and staff members to maximize health and wellness in the school community.

The school infirmary is equipped with the basic materials for first aid and facilities to address the health needs of learners while in school. An on-site trained and qualified nurse is available to manage and assess any health issues that may arise during school hours.

Sports


At R M Public School, we lay strong emphasis on sports and physical development. Our sports curriculum is planned and designed for students to develop and enhance skills like strength, speed, endurance, flexibility, balance etc. We believe in mass participation, exposing our students to a number of sports events at inter-school, state, national and international level. Every student is encouraged to join at least one school sport team such as Basketball, Football, Taekwondo, Cricket, Badminton and indoor sports like Chess, Carom and Table Tennis. Taekwondo gives mental and physical strength. It gives a sense of self-discipline at an early stage of life. Every child’s fantasy begins with being the next Lionel Messy. Football is the most popular sport and every child must have that experience.


We have dedicated football teams along with guided tutoring. Cricket helps improve a child’s agility, judgment and adds on to their team contribution. Basketball adds on to the development of a child’s mind, tolerance, coordination and sense of rigour. Kabaddi helps students for better eye co-ordination and physical strength. These activities stretch their abilities and enhance their stamina, muscle tone and have a number of physical benefits.
